For two days,
PowerShell experts deliver some great content.

Join us for the first PowerShell Conference Asia held in Singapore bought to you by Sapien technologies. during this two day event, powershell speakers from asia and around the world will come together to bring attendees two days of great in depth powershell content. the speakers will be populated on this site shortly, you can expect a strong line up including mvp's, well known community contributors and the powershell team themselves, covering in-depth topics of powershell itself, in addition to how you can use powershell to automate different technologies you work with.

This event has been made possible by SAPIEN technologies, our platinum sponsor! SAPIEN have long supported the PowerShell community and make some of the best tools on the market. With amazing folks like David Corales, Ferdinand Rios and June Blender, SAPIEN are hugely influential in the PowerShell community, we can't thank them enough for helping to make this event happen. SAPIEN will be present for both days and we look forward to welcoming them.

The conference will be held on a Friday and Saturday, minimising the time you need to take off work and to travel. You can arrive in Singapore on the Thursday, take some time in the evening to adjust and see the sights, perhaps join us for a social dinner, then on Friday morning we start the conference. This is likely to include a hackathon on Friday (late afternoon to early evening) and we will reconvene on Saturday morning for the second day. After another day of content we will have a closing session of drinks and nibbles at a local bar leaving you with Sunday to do some more sightseeing and travel home. Sunday is the day of the Formula 1 Grand Prix Night Race, if you are staying on in Singapore we hope to have some race tickets to give away, stay tuned!
